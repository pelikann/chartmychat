    We value your privacy and we do not misuse your personal information.

    How to download chat data from facebook
    1
    2
    1
    2
    3
    1. Open your facebook app (mobile or browser) and go to settings
    2. Scroll down to the subsection "Your Facebook Information" and click on "Download Your Information"
    3. Deselect all the options excepts the messages, then scroll down
    4. • Under the "Data Range" select "All of my data"
      • Under "Format" select "JSON" !!! - important part, your messages will not be analyzed in HTML format, so please make sure that the JSON format is selected
      • Under "Media Quality" select "Low" quality
      • Finally hit the "CREATE FILE" button
    5. • After creating your file, your request will appear in "AVAILABLE COPIES" tab, it might take up to couple of hours until facebook finishes preparing your copy.
      • You will be notified when the file is ready for you to download
